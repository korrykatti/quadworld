About Red Flare 1

Red Flare 1 is the first rocket planned in our QuadWorld Rockets. It has been simulated 10+ times and has flown successfully in simulation. We expect it to launch it by end of 2025. The features of the rocket are as following :

1) 2 stage to orbit
2) Kerolox Fuelling
3) Delta V : 5000m/s
4) Thrust : 1200 Kg
5) Propellent Mass : 13 kg
6) Height : 23 metres
7) Apogee : 260 km
8) Perigee : 130 km
