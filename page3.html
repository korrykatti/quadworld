About Us

Who are we ?

We are a group of students aiming to launch rockets into space at low cost and establish a private rocket company .

How do I contact you?

You can always contact us by clicking the contacts option on the page top menu or contact us using social media.

Can I launch my satellite with you?

Yes, After we successfully launch our Red Flare 1 you can register at our site to launch with us and reach the skies.

What is our Aim?

Our aim is to make spaceflight cheaper and enable humans to become multi-planetary species and reach out the stars .

When will you launch?

We expect to launch our rocket till the end of 2025 as our team is comprised of students still in their high schools
